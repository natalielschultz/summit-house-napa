## Cadence

- **Initial pitch:** send Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11am Pacific (peak open rate for editorial inboxes)
- **Follow-up #1:** 10 business days after initial, single sentence — "wanted to make sure this didn't get buried"
- **Follow-up #2:** 10 business days after #1, share one new angle/detail, then move to "cold"
- **Cool-off:** 6 months before re-pitching the same editor with a new angle

## Per-quarter goal

- Q1: 5 pitches sent, 1 published feature
- Q2: 8 pitches sent, 2 published features
- Q3+: monthly cadence, 1 published feature/month average

A single Dwell or Cup of Jo feature can move the GEO Brand Authority score
from 22 to ~40 in one cycle. Treat each "yes" as compound interest on the
entity graph.
