# Generic Pitch Template — Adapt for any outlet

Use this as a starting point for any travel / design / lifestyle outlet
that isn't covered by a dedicated template. The structure works for boutique
travel blogs, regional publications, and design newsletters.

**Customize before sending:**
- `[Outlet]` — the publication name
- `[Editor First Name]` — pull from masthead or LinkedIn
- `[Reference recent piece]` — name a specific recent article (proves you read them)
- `[Angle hook]` — one sentence tying Summit House to their editorial sweet spot

---

## Subject line patterns (pick one)

- Pitch: [angle hook] at Summit House Napa
- For [Outlet]: 1969 A-frame at the summit of Mount Veeder
- A different way to stay in Napa Valley — story idea

## Body — ~150 words

Hi [Editor First Name],

I really enjoyed [reference recent piece], and wanted to share a story idea
that might fit [Outlet].

Summit House Napa is a private 1969 A-frame at the summit of Mount Veeder
in Napa Valley, offered exclusively for 31-night-minimum residencies. Three
bedrooms on several private acres of ancient redwoods at 1,800 feet, with
hot tub, infrared sauna, meditation trail, and Starlink internet —
designed for extended stays in wine country.

I think it could fit [angle hook — e.g., "your design tour series", "a
Northern California travel piece", "a slow-living feature on extended
stays"]. The 1969 architecture, the renovation story, the Mount Veeder
geography, and the 31-night model are each distinct angles depending on
what's most useful to you.

Full media kit, high-resolution photography, and key facts at
summithousenapa.com/press. Happy to coordinate a press stay during a
quieter residency window (typically Dec–Feb).

Warmly,
The Summit House Napa team
stay@summithousenapa.com

## Ask (pick what fits the outlet)

- Standalone feature
- Inclusion in any [topic] roundup
- House tour / design tour
- Recommendation to a contributor who covers this beat
- Press visit during low-occupancy window
