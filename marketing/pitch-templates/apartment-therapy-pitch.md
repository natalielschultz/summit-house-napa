# Apartment Therapy Pitch — Design / Renovation

**Likely contacts:** Tours Editor, Real Estate Editor, House Tour team.
Apartment Therapy runs a steady cadence of "House Tour" features and design
articles centered on distinctive renovations.

**Best angle:** Renovation house tour. Apartment Therapy's audience loves
specific design details, before/after material choices, and the story of
how a place came to be — without requiring famous owners or Pulitzer-level
interiors. The 1969 A-frame angle plus a modern restoration is on-brand.

---

## Subject line variants

1. House tour pitch: restored 1969 A-frame at the summit of Mount Veeder
2. Mount Veeder A-frame renovation — house tour idea
3. Renovation tour: 1969 Napa A-frame, 2026 restoration

## Body — ~150 words

Hi [Editor First Name],

I'd love to pitch Summit House Napa as a House Tour candidate. It's a 1969
A-frame at the summit of Mount Veeder in Napa Valley, fully renovated in 2026,
and I think the design story is the kind of specificity Apartment Therapy
readers respond to.

The original architecture — three joined A-frames sharing a sunken conversation
pit and a river-rock hearth — was preserved. The renovation focused on
materials (cedar, stone, brass, sage palette), a chef's kitchen with a
custom backsplash, and an outdoor wellness suite (hot tub, infrared sauna,
outdoor shower) that flows into the redwood forest behind the house. There's
also a meditation trail on the property. Many distinctive details to feature
visually.

High-resolution photography and a media kit are at
summithousenapa.com/press. Happy to coordinate a press stay if a House Tour
photographer wants to visit in person.

Warmly,
The Summit House Napa team
stay@summithousenapa.com

House Tour feature OR inclusion in any A-frame / mid-century renovation
roundup. Open to providing photographer access during low-occupancy windows.
