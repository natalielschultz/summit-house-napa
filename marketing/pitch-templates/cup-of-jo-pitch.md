# Cup of Jo Pitch — Slow living / family rental

**Likely contacts:** Joanna Goddard or features team. Cup of Jo runs intimate,
first-person friendly stories about places and people.

**Best angle:** Cup of Jo loves "we did this thing for a month" stories from
real families and creative people. The 31-night model lends itself to a
"what it's like to spend a month here" piece more than a typical hotel review.
Strong design and slow-living elements both register with Joanna's audience.

---

## Subject line variants

1. A month at a 1969 A-frame in Napa — pitch
2. Place pitch: monthly residency at a Mount Veeder A-frame
3. Slow Napa: pitch for a family month-stay feature

## Body — ~150 words

Hi [Joanna / Editor First Name],

Long-time reader. Two things Cup of Jo often does well that made me think of
Summit House Napa: pieces about families taking a month somewhere, and
intimate place writing about distinctive design.

Summit House is a 1969 A-frame at the summit of Mount Veeder in Napa Valley —
three bedrooms, sleeps 11, on several private acres of ancient redwoods.
It's exclusively for 31-night residencies, which means most guests treat it
as a season, not a getaway. Sunken conversation pit, hot tub under the stars,
meditation trail through the redwoods, full kitchen for cooking real meals
with real groceries. Starlink works for the parent who needs to be on calls.
Best moments tend to be Sunday-morning quiet ones, not Saturday-night ones.

Happy to share photography, the media kit, or arrange a stay if you or a
contributor would like to write from the inside.
summithousenapa.com/press

Warmly,
The Summit House Napa team
stay@summithousenapa.com

## Ask

A "we spent a month at..." style feature, ideally written from a contributor's
real stay. Cup of Jo is most powerful when it's first-person and lived-in.
Press visits coordinated for slow seasons (Dec–Feb).
